Frequently Asked Questions

How does Privasim check links?

We analyze URL structure, domain reputation, SSL security, and known malicious patterns.

Is my link stored or tracked?

No. All scans are private and links are never saved.

What does the risk percentage mean?

It shows the likelihood that a link may contain phishing, malware, or unsafe redirects.

Is Privasim free to use?

Yes, basic link verification is completely free.
